HOD's Message

Agriculture science is a dynamic subject enhancing students with management of agricultural enterprise and enlightens students on issues which have become a global phenomenon like food security and climate change. This is a practical-oriented subject which not only enables students to comprehend crop cultivation and livestock rearing techniques but also develop understanding of sustainable farming techniques and management aspects of the farm. As we are battling the fight against the pandemic, the onus is on us as educationist to bring forward the importance of this important sector of the economy which is almost neglected by many. We certainly deal with adolescents whose minds are ready to absorb good ideas and interpret things on their own with some or little guidance. The idea of stable agriculture based on a tradition which is transmitted from generation to generation, has been gradually replaced by the image of modern agricultural enterprise, whose rapid revolution is propelled by progress of science and technology.
